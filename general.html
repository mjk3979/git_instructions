Frequently Asked Questions

"I have made a grave error. How do I reset everything??"

There are two possible courses of action, depending on your needs:

  • If you want to keep the changes you've made since you're last commit, you can unstage everything:
    $ git reset
  • If you want to discard your changes and start fresh, simply revert everything to the last commit:
    $ git reset --hard
